Whoo, up.front #31! We have two exciting talks by Gunnar and Martijn. Also Rachel, organizer of HTML5BUG, will stop by and present her brand new usergroup and Nicolò will give a short demo of Board. We are looking forward to seeing you on November 13, 7:30 pm at co.up.

  • Martijn Schreuder: Designing for the way they shape our world

    All over the world, charities and social initiatives make sure we have a clean, social and livable today and tomorrow. And sometimes they reach the boundaries of the expertise that their members can provide or that they can afford. Providing such expertise pro bono can be a deciding push forward. The GiveCamp is meant to provide such expertise in a specific field: technology and design. Ten Berlin non-profits will meet at RallyPad for one weekend with 30-50 developers and designers to create market quality solutions.

    Martijn is a neuroscientist at TU-Berlin, founder of pro bono platform Talentspender and co-organizer of the first GiveCamp here in Berlin.

  • Gunnar Bittersmann: CSS preprocessors to do the dirty work

    Download Slides

    This talk covers how CSS preprocessors can help with what every web developer hates: supporting legacy IE, and with what every web devoloper should hate: OOCSS. He’ll discuss its downsides and how we could do better to achieve its goals.

    Gunnar is a frontend developer at brands4friends, helps organizing the annual World Usability Day Berlin, and translates articles on the W3C i18n website.
